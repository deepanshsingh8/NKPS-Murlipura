### Affiliation board — important nuance

- The Murlipura campus is the **original/founding** location (1985). The school is repeatedly described as **CBSE, English medium**, and offers Class X & XII board exams.
- **However**, the **examination page** of this English-medium site states the school "conducts examinations aligned with Rajasthan's educational framework" (Frist Term, Second Term, Half Yearly, Third Term, Annual). That is RBSE-style terminology, not CBSE.
- The CBSE affiliation we *can* prove on SARAS (CBSE's official portal) belongs to the **Rajawas, Sikar Road** branch:
  - **CBSE Affiliation No.** `1730406`
  - Address: Rajawas, Sikar Road, Jaipur 302013
  - Founded: 2005 (school opening 16 Apr 2007)
  - Managing Organisation: **Bhartiya Samajik Vikas Samiti**
  - Affiliation period: 01/04/2025 – 31/03/2030
  - Principal (Rajawas): Aradhana Pareek, M.A., B.Ed.
- **For Murlipura**, no separate CBSE affiliation number is visible on the public web. The school self-describes as CBSE in directories (ezyschooling, Vidya Setu, Careers360, Justdial), but the original site's own examination page reads RBSE. **Verify with school directly before publishing the affiliation board on the new site.** Safest copy for the new site: *"Affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) — affiliation number to be confirmed."*
- The Rajawas branch's CBSE affiliation should NOT be reused for Murlipura on the new site.

### Mission statement (verbatim from `/page/mission-vision`)
> "To be a center of excellence in education which, in keeping with the rich heritage of india, will stress the simultaneous development of body, mind and spirit, and endeavour to create compassionate, responsible and innovative global citizens who are committed to the development of India."

### Vision statement (verbatim)
> "To prepare dynamic and caring citizens of tomorrow to meet the challenges of a global society while retaining their traditional values."

### About the school (paraphrased from the Welcome / Knowledge-of-School pages)
- Established in 1985 under the guidance of Late Shri R. K. Choudhary.
- Started with 10 students; the group has now crossed 4,000+ students.
- Founder served in the Indian armed forces and was decorated for service.
- After the founder's death in 2005, the school continues "on his ideals and legacy" — values of discipline, education, and human values.
- Attracts students from multiple states and (per the site) internationally.
- Self-described as "one of the finest schools in Northern Jaipur" (from MD's message).

## 5. Academics

### Classes offered
- **Nursery, LKG, UKG** (pre-primary)
- **Class I — Class V** (primary)
- **Class VI — Class VIII** (middle)
- **Class IX — Class X** (secondary)
- **Class XI — Class XII** with **Science** and **Commerce** streams (senior secondary)
- Coeducational throughout.

### Curriculum / examinations
- The dedicated `/page/curricula` slug returns 404 — content is not currently available on the live site.
- Examination policy page (`/page/examination`):
  - Five-assessment year: First Term, Second Term, Half Yearly, Third Term, Annual.
  - **Minimum 75% attendance** required for final exams.
  - No re-examinations for absences; medical certificate needed for illness.
  - All dues must be cleared before final exams.
  - Exam dates announced separately.

### Scholarship schemes (`/page/scholarship-schemes`)
Merit-based, paid in two installments (split across Class XI and XII). Eligibility based on Class X board %:

| Class X % | Reward |
|---|---|
| 87.00% – 89.99% | ₹2,100 one-time |
| 90.00% – 95.00% | 25% tuition-fee reduction (split XI + XII) |
| 95.01% – 97.00% | 50% tuition-fee reduction (split XI + XII) |
| 97.01% – 100.00% | 100% tuition-fee waiver (split XI + XII) |

- Second installment paid only if the student secures **≥ 80%** in Class XI.
- A student who leaves after Class XI must repay the received scholarship before the TC is issued.

### Student council (`/page/student-council`)
- Prefects council with school captain, house captains, head boy/girl, games captains.
- Selected by faculty and house staff.
- Functions: discipline, ethical guidance, school honour, pastoral care; in-house counsellors help students manage academic pressure and emotional development.

## 7. Admissions

### Process
- Applications submitted on the prescribed form (included with prospectus).
- Birth certificate required with application (except Nursery).
- Transfer Certificate + previous-school marksheet required from Class II onwards.
- Registration fee is **non-refundable** and does **not** guarantee admission.
- Parents must deposit fees within **3 days** of admission approval.

### Age criteria (as on 1 April)
| Class | Minimum age |
|---|---|
| Nursery | 3+ years |
| L.K.G. | 4+ years |
| U.K.G. | 5+ years |
| Class I | 6+ years |

### Evaluation
- **Nursery — Class I:** Parent/guardian interaction.
- **Class II and above:** Written entrance exam (Hindi, English, Mathematics) + interview.
- Selection on "first come, first serve" with priority for local residents.
- **RTE Act:** 25% reservation in Nursery and Class I.

### Documents required
- Birth certificate (mandatory).
- Transfer certificate (Class II onwards).
- Previous school marksheet (Class II onwards).
- Photographs.
- Completed application form.

### Fee structure
- **Not published on the website.** Prospectus download link: `https://www.nkpublicschool.org/english/download_prospectus`.
